FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Yes, IEC (Import Export Code) is mandatory for any individual or entity that wishes to import or export goods and services from India. It is issued by DGFT and has lifetime validity with no renewal requirement.
With Yuva Consultancy, IEC registration typically takes 1-2 working days after submission of all required documents. The process is done entirely online through the DGFT portal.
IEC and RCMC are separate documents. While IEC enables basic import/export, RCMC is required to avail export incentives, duty exemptions, and government export promotion schemes. It is issued by Export Promotion Councils.
AD Code (Authorised Dealer Code) is a 14-digit code issued by your bank and registered at the customs port. Without AD Code registration at port, exporters cannot receive foreign inward remittance against their export shipments.
Absolutely! Any individual, proprietorship, partnership, LLP, or private limited company can export from India. You just need a valid IEC, GSTIN, and a bank account. We help businesses of all sizes set up for export.
