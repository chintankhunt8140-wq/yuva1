FSSAI Registration

FSSAI (Food Safety and Standards Authority of India) registration is mandatory for any food business operator including manufacturers, distributors, retailers, importers, and exporters of food in India.

We handle your FSSAI application end-to-end — from determining the right license type to submission and follow-up.

Apply for FSSAI →

Documents Required

  • Aadhaar Card & PAN Card of proprietor
  • Passport size photograph
  • Proof of business address
  • Nature of business & food category
  • Bank account details

Benefits

  • Legal authorisation to operate food business
  • Enhanced consumer trust and brand credibility
  • Eligibility for export of food products
  • Avoidance of heavy fines & penalties

DSC — Digital Signature Certificate

A Digital Signature Certificate (DSC) is the digital equivalent of a physical signature and is essential for e-filing income tax returns, ROC filing, GST registration, and tendering on government portals.

We provide Class 2 & Class 3 DSC for individuals, directors, and organisations with doorstep delivery.

Get DSC Now →

Documents Required

  • Aadhaar Card & PAN Card
  • Passport size photograph
  • Email ID & Mobile number
  • Video KYC verification (Class 3)

Types Available

  • Class 2 DSC — Individual & Organisation
  • Class 3 DSC — For e-tendering, import/export
  • Document Signer DSC — Bulk signing
  • DGFT DSC — For DGFT portal filings
Starting from ₹999

IEC — Import Export Code

Import Export Code (IEC) is a 10-digit unique code issued by DGFT (Directorate General of Foreign Trade) and is mandatory for any business that imports or exports goods and services from India.

No IEC = No import or export. We help you get your IEC within 1-2 working days.

Apply for IEC →

Documents Required

  • PAN Card of firm/entity
  • Aadhaar / Voter ID / Passport of proprietor
  • Cancelled cheque / Bank certificate
  • Digital Signature Certificate
  • GSTIN (if applicable)

Benefits

  • Lifetime validity — no renewal required
  • Access to DGFT export promotion schemes
  • Eligibility for MEIS & RoDTEP benefits
  • Required for all customs clearances
Starting from ₹1,999

AD Code Registration

Authorised Dealer Code (AD Code) is a 14-digit unique code issued by your bank and registered at the port of export. It is mandatory for receiving foreign inward remittance against export shipments.

IFSC Registration

The Indian Financial System Code (IFSC) is an alphanumeric 11-digit code used to identify bank branches for NEFT, RTGS, and IMPS transactions — essential for international trade and receiving foreign payments efficiently.

Register IFSC →

When You Need It

  • Receiving export payment remittances
