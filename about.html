Our Story

From Surat to the World Stage

Yuva Consultancy was founded with a singular mission: to make business compliance simple, affordable, and accessible for every Indian entrepreneur. What started as a small consulting practice in Surat has grown into a trusted firm serving thousands of businesses across India and internationally.

We specialise in navigating the complex landscape of Indian regulations — from FSSAI and IEC to international company formation in USA, UK, UAE, and beyond. Our team of experienced CAs, legal professionals, and compliance experts bring unmatched depth of knowledge to every client engagement.
